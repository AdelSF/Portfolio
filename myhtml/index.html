About the Artist

My name is Adel Paakraad, I was born in Iran, I have been art student for almost all my life, started from working with my father on Murals as a kid then practice drawing during the college, I studied in film school for about 2 years and have experience in drawing and painting more than 5 years. you can see some of my drawing on the Drawing Section

Then I started to paint with Oil Color and worked as a painting teacher

I always was passionate about Drawing Prtraites but my passion for Photography started from film school when I was facenated with the portrait photography and I started to learn more about it more, when I moved to the U.S. I decided to follow my passion and work around it.

My goal always was to show the beauti in the daliy life and ordinary people. I learned during the work with different models dring many years and I tried to share my own point about with people. check the photography section to see some of my works.

I work as software engneer and the website that you currently see, is designed and bulit by me.
